Regional Meeting — 2024

EGAP Regional Meeting

The EGAP Regional Meeting gathered experimental researchers from across the Americas to share work-in-progress and debate methodology. The format — small groups, intensive feedback, no spectators — produced some of the sharpest critiques and most generous suggestions I've received on early-stage projects.

I left with new collaborators, a longer reading list, and a much clearer sense of where my own research fits within the broader political-economy conversation in Latin America.
