Hi! I'm Brayan A. Condori, an economist from Juliaca, in the Peruvian Andes. I am currently pursuing a Master's Degree in Economics at Universidad Nacional de La Plata (Argentina) and hold a BA in Economics from Universidad de Piura (Peru).

Previously, I worked as a Research Analyst at Innovations for Poverty Action (IPA), where I contributed to field experiments on intimate partner violence, school management, and STEAM education in Peru and Belize.

I plan to eventually complete a PhD in Economics or another related social science.
