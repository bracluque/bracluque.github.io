RIDGE Summer School

LACEA Conference — RIDGE Summer School

The RIDGE Summer School at LACEA is one of the best places in the region for advanced training in development economics. Lectures from leading scholars, hands-on sessions on identification, and intensive discussions with peers all combined to push my thinking on the projects I was working on at the time.

What stays with me most is the intellectual generosity — senior economists giving real, careful feedback to early-career researchers, and a cohort that supported each other across institutions and countries.
