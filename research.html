Selected Work from IPA Experience

Field Experiments on Intimate Partner Violence Prevention

Innovations for Poverty Action | Peru
Add project image
Project Summary Methodology Data
Abstract
Contributed to the design and implementation of randomized controlled trials evaluating interventions to prevent intimate partner violence in Peru. Responsibilities included survey design, data collection coordination, and preliminary statistical analysis using Stata. The project aimed to understand the effectiveness of community-based interventions in reducing domestic violence incidents and changing social norms around gender-based violence.

Impact Evaluation of School Management Interventions

Innovations for Poverty Action | Peru
Add education project image
Technical Report Data Analysis Policy Brief
Abstract
Evaluated the impact of school management training programs on educational outcomes in rural Peru. Conducted extensive fieldwork, managed data collection processes across multiple school districts, and performed statistical analysis using advanced econometric techniques in Stata. The study focused on measuring improvements in student performance, teacher effectiveness, and administrative efficiency following the implementation of management training programs.

Cross-Country Evaluation of STEAM Education Programs

Innovations for Poverty Action | Peru and Belize
Add cross-country project image
Cross-Country Analysis Policy Brief Comparative Results
Abstract
Led a comparative evaluation of Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics (STEAM) education programs across Peru and Belize. Coordinated data collection across multiple countries, harmonized survey instruments, and conducted cross-country statistical comparisons. The project examined the differential impacts of STEAM interventions across diverse cultural and economic contexts, providing insights for scaling educational innovations in Latin America and Central America.
