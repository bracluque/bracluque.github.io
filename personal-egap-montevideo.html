Uruguay — 2024

EGAP Learning Days: Montevideo

EGAP's Learning Days workshop in Montevideo brought together early-career researchers and senior scholars working at the intersection of governance and experimental methods. Over several days we worked through pre-analysis plans, ethical considerations, and the tradeoffs of different RCT designs — feedback that has directly shaped my own ongoing projects.

What made Montevideo special was the network. Conversations begun in seminar rooms continued in cafés along the Rambla, and many of the people I met there are now collaborators or close friends in the field.
